Facilities

Classes from Nursery to 10th

Our school offers education from Nursery up to 10th Standard. Each class is guided by experienced teachers dedicated to nurturing students academically and personally.

Learning Rooms & Labs

We have a well-equipped science lab, VR lab, computer lab, drawing and craft rooms, and a library, encouraging creative, digital, and scientific learning experiences.

Hygiene & Clean Water

Separate washrooms for boys and girls are available on each floor. For health and hygiene, R.O. water purifiers ensure clean drinking water throughout the school.

Playground & Indoor Games

The school has a large ground with a volleyball court, kabaddi mat, and space for various outdoor and indoor games, promoting physical fitness and teamwork.

FCF Training

We provide regular FCF (Future Combat Force) training every week, helping students build self-defense skills, discipline, and physical strength.

Security & Kid-Friendly Zone

CCTV cameras are installed in all corridors and classrooms. A safe park area with slides and a climbing wall is specially designed for our younger students.

Cycle Parking

We have a dedicated empty area for parking bicycles, encouraging eco-friendly travel among students while ensuring safety and order.

School Transport & Security

Our school provides bus services for students. For added security, watchmen are stationed at the gates, ensuring a safe environment throughout the day.
